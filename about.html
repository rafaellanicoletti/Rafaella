About Me



As a seasoned UX/UI and Product Designer, I offer a diverse skill set honed through years of experience across various platforms, including desktop, mobile, and enterprise-level SaaS systems. My expertise lies in translating complex platforms into responsive screens while maintaining optimal flow and design patterns, coupled with a knack for incorporating engaging animations to enhance user interaction. In previous roles, I've played a pivotal role in collaborative design system development, crafting assets and components that adhere to modern design standards and accessibility guidelines. My portfolio includes designing prototypes for the healthcare industry and thriving in Agile environments, where I've consistently delivered results that drive customer engagement and elevate conversion rates. Notably, my contributions have led to a remarkable 100% retention rate over two years. Passionate about modern UI/UX patterns and a user-centric approach, I excel in integrating responsive design seamlessly from wireframes to prototypes. My remote work experience with diverse teams has further refined my collaborative skills, while my enthusiasm for continuous learning and exploration fuels my creativity both personally and professionally. With five years of proven experience as a UX designer, I approach each project with adaptability, enthusiasm, and a keen eye for detail. Beyond my professional endeavors, I'm an avid learner and traveler, always seeking new inspiration to infuse into my design work. I am eager to collaborate with your team and contribute to the success of future projects by transforming product vision into tangible design strategies.


I try to spend as much time as possible in France while learning the language. I love to spend the summer in Cannes. You can usually find me reading about art or how to learn a new language in the least amount of time. I am also passionate about airplanes and boats and one day would love to get both a pilot and boat licence.
