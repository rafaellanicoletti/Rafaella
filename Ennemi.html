UI PROJECT / ENNEMI

Prohibition no more! Find the coolest hidden gems in your city and celebrate with a drink!

MOODBOARD



This project is part of a small UI exercise for the CALARTS UI Visual Elements course part of the UI/UX Specialization. I was required to come up with a concept and different UI elements that you can check out below. I was inspired by the Speakeasies in New York City and the prohibition era. Ennemi is an app to find Speakeasies in your city. Designed for drinkers, young adults and cocktail connoisseurs. The app works with a Google maps interface that will show your location and icons will pop up if there is a speakeasy near you. Ennemi stands for ‘Enemy’ which alcohol was, in the prohibition and that’s why speakeasies started.
