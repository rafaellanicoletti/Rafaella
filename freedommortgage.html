Freedom Mortgage

Overall tasks

At Freedom Mortgage, I worked on redesigning and creating customer-facing tools on the .com experience as well as integrating responsive design, there we worked in Agile environment in 1-month sprints with an entire Product team that collaborated closely with Developers and the Marketing department. We worked with InVision, Sketch, Confluence and Jira. We had a UX Team of about 10-14 people in different areas of expertise back office, .com (the one I was in), Mobile, Design System and Research.



Customer Reviews

In the main page at Freedom Mortgage there was a Customer Reviews module that only displayed 5 stars from Freedom Mortgage only reviews.


Problem & Goals

The main issue was the lack of trust we presented to prospective clients. All reviews were 5 stars and the only source they came from was from our own website. This is why we were required to partner up with Credit Karma or Nerd Wallet to display our score of their respective users. The Goal was to design a module that followed the patterns of the website but integrating new colors and assets from an external vendor, in this case, Nerd Wallet. We also had to display a more truthful score that would make our user trust us more.


Exploratory Research and Wireframes

For the exploratory research we needed to get a feel of trustworthiness our customers had in terms of those scores. Also, we were able to ask the appropiate questions to really know what could make better this customer reviews module. Some of the exploratory questions were for people to score our existing module, if they trust the external vendor (which one in that case, Credit Karma or Nerd Wallet), What is their opinion on 5 star reviews, etc.
