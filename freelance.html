The Process

BRIEF

Here we will take time to understand what are your needs and goals for the project. We can include unlimited meetings and calls to make sure we have a good foundation to go from. This will allow us to create a great product for the user and your specific needs and goals.


RESEARCH

This phase I'll get to know your user, this means understanding its needs, goals, wants, context and behaviors. Some methods I can include are 1:1 interviews, Market Research, User testing, card sorting and focus groups. If you have other preferred methods, I'll be happy to include them as well.


DESIGN

Everything we have learned until this point will be used to start building ideas. I begin with user flows and sitemap of the product, this will be followed by wireframes to start setting up an idea and work from there. As we go along, mid or high fidelity design I can get valuable feedback to start tweaking to ultimate build the best product for you.


TEST & ITERATE

Through testing and iteration, we will ensure the prototype meets expectations, user needs, business goals and what we have discused previously as well.
