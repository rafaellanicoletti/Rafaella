F&E MEDIA

Video of one of the last prototypes



F&E Media is a company based in Chile that offers Press Clipping services to other organizations. Their main clients are associations/organizations based in Santiago, Chile. Above you can see one of the last prototypes of the static website of F&E Media (their commercial name is PrensaClipp). What we needed to understand it was what F&E Media stakeholders wanted and needed and also catch the needs of organizations when interacting with websites when they are looking for a specific service (Like Press Clippings). This website is directed to organizations who are looking to have a better online/news presence and it can also work well on measuring the public's reaction to them. Therefore, this siter is more of an informative site to gain new customers and to also provide reputation to F&E Media.
