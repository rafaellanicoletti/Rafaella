TIMER APP

Currently in Development




IDEAS & GOALS

There is a need of interval timers that don't interfere with music. More often than not when you go to another app your music stops which is not what we are looking for. The benefits of this app is it doesn't interfere with your favorite music streaming apps on your phone. It is easy to use.




Problem - Solution - Thoughts

It was really hard to find a timer app that would allow you to play music while in the app without interrupting the stopwatch. Because of the pandemic a lot of us have started working out at home. I have been working out at home for 8 months already and I do have a specific routine that requires set times. At the beginning I didn’t mind too much stopping my workout to see how many seconds were left but after a couple of weeks I just lost the focus on my workouts and it became harder to stay on top with it. I searched everywhere for an app that would show me the timer, would actually make a noise and wouldn’t interrupt my Spotify paid playlist. Of course, I didn’t find one. What I wanted to accomplish with this app was something easy to use. That anyone would be able to use and the colors and the feel of the app would represent an upbeat attitude.



Challenge

The main challenge of this app was making it simple yet interesting for the user. I didn’t want just a stopwatch like the iPhone gives. I wanted to show some statistics so the user could know more how he/she interacts with app.
